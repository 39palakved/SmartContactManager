About Smart Contact Manager

Effortlessly manage your contacts with cloud-based solutions.

Introduction

The Contact Book project, developed by Palak Ved, is a powerful solution designed to simplify the management of contacts. Whether you're adding new contacts, syncing them with cloud storage, or updating their information, our platform ensures that all your contact information is easily accessible and securely stored in the cloud.

Key Features

  • Add, edit, and delete contacts easily
  • Sync contact details and images with the cloud (via Cloudinary)
  • View all your contacts in a structured, searchable format
  • Fully responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Secure data storage with integration into the cloud

Benefits

Our Contact Book solution offers the best of both worlds—simplicity and security. It’s perfect for users who want quick access to their contacts across multiple devices, with all data securely stored in the cloud. The interface is user-friendly, making it ideal for both personal and professional use.

Technology Stack

This project leverages cutting-edge technologies to provide a seamless and secure experience:

  • Spring Boot & Java: Power the back-end with robust and scalable services.
  • MySQL: Manage and store contact information in a relational database.
  • Cloudinary: Cloud service for secure storage and management of contact images.
  • Tailwind CSS: Modern and responsive design for a great user experience on any device.

Future Goals

We’re committed to constantly improving the Contact Book platform. Some upcoming features include:

  • Mobile app version for Android and iOS
  • Integration with third-party services like Google Contacts and Microsoft Outlook
  • Advanced contact filtering and tagging system
  • Improved analytics and insights for professional users
